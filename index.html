Instructions to placing the Purifier

Electrical Restrictions

Flooring Restrictions

Surroundings Restriction

Electrical Restrictions

Plug the appliance directly into a 120 V AC electrical outlet

DO NOT place humidifier near any such heat sources

Never plae anything over moisture outlet when the unit is running.

Flooring Restrictions

To avoid Fire Hazard, NEVER put the appliance or its cords under Rugs.

Always place Humidifier on a firm, flat, level surface.

Surrounding Restrictions

NEVER place humidifier in an area where it is accessible to to Children.

DO NOT use Outdoors.

Do not use Humidifier in an area where humidity level is in excess of 50% (tested by a Hygrometer)
